MagCase Series

Magnetic card cases designed for collectors who want easy access without the commitment of grading.

The Problem with Existing Solutions

Most card cases fall into two categories: cheap top-loaders that offer zero protection and premium one-touch cases that are expensive, bulky, and a pain to open repeatedly. If you wanted to show off your best ungraded cards that are too nice to leave in a binder—your options were limited and frustrating.

The MagCase Design

The MagCase system uses embedded magnets to secure your cards without the hassle of screw-down cases or the flimsiness of snap cases. You can open it one-handed, swap the card, and close it in seconds. The magnetic seal is strong enough to keep the card secured but smooth enough that you're not fighting to open it.

What makes this system unique is the modularity. Every MagCase has interchangeable top and bottom pieces. Want your Charizard in a red and orange case? Or a Squirtle with light blue and beige? You can mix and match colors to complement the card inside. I haven't seen anyone else doing this.

Who This Is For

MagCases work with any trading card that fits a standard top-loader: Pokemon, sports cards, Yu-Gi-Oh, Magic: The Gathering. They're built for collectors who have cards they're proud of but never sent out for professional grading. MagCases give you a display-quality presentation without the commitment or cost of grading services.
