How to Use

Adding Products

  • Click any product card below the canvas to add items to the shelf
  • For the MagCase, select a color before clicking to add

Interacting with Objects

  • Click an object on the shelf to select it (it will glow green)
  • Drag selected objects left or right to rearrange their position
  • Click the background to deselect
  • Double-click an object to view product details and Etsy link

Camera Controls

  • Drag the background to rotate the camera view

Organization Tools

  • Clear All: Remove all objects from the shelf at once
